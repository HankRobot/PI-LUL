Welcome to BC-LUL's Prop Coin Website

"Rethinking Property Investment-Easy,safe and available to everyone. Another project brought to you by BC-LUL"

PropCoin is the new way to invest in property, by tokenizing each property, we aim to crowdfund property purchases, such that 
everyone can buy a fraction of the property and enjoy returns when the properties are sold. 

PropCoin is developed as a prototype during the NEM Merdeka Hackathon held at the NEM Blockchain Center on 16th September 2018
